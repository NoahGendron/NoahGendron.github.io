Projects

Age of Conquest

Age of Conquest is a medieval themed mix of MMORPG and Action-Adventure. The project was developed in Roblox Studio over the span of four years by a very small team, of which I was the main programmer and designer. The game released in december of 2024 and peaked at around 140 concurrent players. The game also reached over 138,000 visits(plays) since.

Outlander

Outlander (Étranger in French), is a fast-paced melee focused 3D roguelike game with procedural generation. It was developed within only four months, by a team of just three people for College Lionel-Groulx's Fall 2023 semester, as the main semester project. The project was developed in Unity and coded in C#.
