Destructo

Destructo est un projet d'équipe réalisé par deux personnes pour la fin de session de mon cours de Développement de Jeux Vidéo créé dans Unity. Le jeu a été développé en tant que prototype/démo en moins d'une semaine.

Overview

The goal of Destructo is to destroy different structures during each level with a limited amount of ammunition.

Demo

Conception et design

Despite its mechanical and visual simplicity, a lot of thought and effort was put into the design of Destructo. To make the game more interesting, every level acts as a puzzle, where the player must find the most optimal solution in order to beat the level. This is achieved with the use of the destruction system, which makes it so certain parts of a structure can be more resilient than others and some other partscan act as weaknesses. In the first level, the player can't destroy the metal slide until the structure reaches half of its maximum hitpoints. Once broken though, the slide acts a spinning, damaging projectile, capable of destroying other parts of the structure. This same system applies to the second level, where the structure is too resilient to be blown up, with only 4 grenades, so the player has to destroy it with the use of the wind turbine next to it.
