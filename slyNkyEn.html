Sly n' Ky: 2-in-1

The development of Sly n' Ky took place during the Ubisoft Game Lab of 2026, you can find and download the game HERE.

Overview

Sly n' Ky: 2-in-1 in is a cooperative 3D platformer and collectathon where each player embodies one of the two ends of an entity connected by a slinky. Each players must progress through the two levels while learning to overcome the constraint of the slinky, but also to use it as an advantage. This slinky limits the distance between the two players, but it can also serve as a spring and an elastic band to propel each other in order to reach heights that would otherwise be inaccessible. According to the rules of the competition, the project had to be playable with at least two players and use only the directional pad as well as two main buttons on the controller, in the same spirit as games from the 80s and 90s.

Role and Contribution

As a designer with extensive experience with Unity and C#, I initially worked very hard on coming up with game concepts and ideas and pitched them with my teammates until we found the best idea for the project. Then, my role as a designer shifted to planning and devising mechanics related to the slinky'S abilities, but also design related to the game's structure and gameplay loop. Once the conceptual aspect of development was finalized, I worked on level design, implementing audio, and some additional programming relating to LDO (Level Design Objects) mostly.
